A sequence
\pstr{
\nd{t = \lambda \cdot}(app){@}
\nd{\cdot}(ly){\lambda y}
\nd{\ldots}(y){y}
\nd{\cdot}(lx){\lambda \overline{x}}
\nd{\ldots}(x){x_i}
\nd{\cdot}(leta){\lambda \overline{\eta_i}} % {\ldots}
\lnk{y}{ly}  {35} {\tiny 1}
\lnk{lx}{app}{38} {\tiny 1}
\lnk{x}{lx}  {30} {\tiny i}
\lnk{leta}{y}{40} {\tiny i}
}.

\getparamComma{}{Targ}{15,2,3}

The same one with the succinct syntax:
\Pstr{
\lambda\cdot (app)               {@}
\cdot        (ly)                {\lambda y}

%{\ldots}     (y-ly,35:{\tiny i1})   {y}

%{\ldots}     (x-lx,30,{\tiny i})   {x_i}
%{\cdot}      (leta-y,40,{\tiny i}) {\lambda \overline{\eta_i} }{\ldots}
}.
